SellForMe Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-06

What We Store

SellForMe stores your account profile, listings, photos, and chat messages on our backend (Supabase) so they can sync across devices. eBay OAuth tokens are stored securely in your device's Keychain.

Third Parties

  • Supabase — backend storage of accounts, listings, and messages.
  • OpenAI — used for AI pricing and negotiation responses. Item details and photos may be sent.
  • eBay — used to research market prices and (optionally) cross-post your listings, with your explicit consent.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party advertising. We do not share data outside the services listed above.
